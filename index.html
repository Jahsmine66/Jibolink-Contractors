About Jibolink

Jibolink Contractor Nigeria Limited is a premier construction, maintenance, and property services company with a reputation for delivering exceptional service quality and customer satisfaction across Nigeria. With over a decade of experience, we have grown to become a trusted partner for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Our range of services is extensive and tailored to meet the evolving needs of our customers. We specialize in professional plumbing, electrical installations, facility management, civil works, and end-to-end building construction. Each of these services is handled by highly skilled professionals who are dedicated to ensuring that every project adheres to the highest standards of quality and safety.
